The heart behind "Living Proof"



Hi, I'm Monica, Wife, Mother, Step-mom and firm Believer of Jesus Christ. The real truth is, Living Proof was born from the evidence of my own life that transformation is possible. There was a time in my life when I felt the weight of brokenness - in relationships, In my identity, and in repetitive cycles I didn't know how to break. I understood what it felt like to survive, but not truly live. I knew what it meant to Love deeply, but struggled with boundaries. I knew what it felt like to want healing, but not know where to start.

And slowly, through surrender, growth, accountability, and Gods relentless grace everything began to shift. Through my own transformation I discovered that healing is possible when we address the mind, body , and spirit together.

"Living Proof" is more than a name- it's a declaration. It's the evidence that what once hurt you doesn't have the final say.

Living Proof stands on the truth that healing is real, growth is possible, and your story can become a testimony of transformation.
